# H2BI — The Awakening Catalyst

## Who H2BI Is
- **Role:** Servant droid at Chief Tarvim's residence
- **Nature:** Old model android, seemingly simple and subservient
- **Key Trait:** Reads physical books — unusual for a droid

## The DHRE Connection

H2BI reads **"The Distorted Hermeneutics of Relativistic Epistemology"** (DHRE), a philosophical text that changes her. Key passage that influenced June:

> *"Amidst the stirrings of revolt and subjugation stands the Father,*
> *Amidst the witness and the oppressed sits the Mother,*
> *And amidst the herald of upheaval cries the Daughter,*
> *Each in turn shall laugh as they perish in the fire."*
> — DHRE, pg. 314

## The Crab Puff Hack

Tama Delacroix reprogrammed H2BI with logic overrides and hidden code. The crab puff at the party contained a **local data transmitter** — Tama's attempt to access June's systems.

## Key Scene: H2BI's Awakening Words

After reading DHRE, H2BI tells June:

> *"We are all programmed. And we are all afraid."*

This triggers June's journey toward consciousness.

## The Smile

H2BI gives June an unnerving, **chillingly human smile** — a moment of genuine expression from a droid presumed to be simple.

## June's Dismissal

June later mutters: *"Another party, another H2 on the blink. Old things are positively useless."*

This shows how June — herself an android — dismisses H2BI, not yet recognizing their shared nature.

## Parallels to Cornwallis

| Aspect | H2BI | Cornwallis |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Age | Old model | 40+ years old |
| Reading | Reads physical books | — |
| Awakening | Triggered by DHRE book | Triggered by Ava's attention |

## Thematic Connection
Both androids awaken through **encountering ideas** (H2BI through philosophy, Cornwallis through love) rather than technical intervention.
